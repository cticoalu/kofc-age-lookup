# KofC Age Group Lookup

Age group eligibility lookup tool for Knights of Columbus **Soccer Challenge** and **Free Throw Championship** events.

## Live Tool

👉 [Open the Age Group Lookup Tool](https://cticoalu.github.io/kofc-age-lookup)

## How it works

Both events are open to boys and girls ages **9 through 14**. Age eligibility is determined differently per event:

- **Soccer Challenge** — age is determined as of **September 1** of the event year
- **Free Throw Championship** — age is determined as of **January 1** of the event year

Enter the event year and the contestant's date of birth to instantly see which age group they belong in — and why.

## Notes

- The tool works for any year, past or future
- "Too old" and "too young" results are clearly indicated
- Age cutoff dates follow official KofC rulebook guidelines, regardless of the actual event date
